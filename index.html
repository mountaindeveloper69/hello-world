About us

Hello! We are the Sharpmap team — mountain enthusiasts, TOPR (Tatra Mountain Rescue Service) and GOPR (Mountain Volunteer Rescue Service) rescuers, and Tatra guides. Thanks to our extensive experience in the mountains, we understand exactly what is needed on the trail. That’s why we created an app that is precise, reliable, and tailored to the realities of mountain terrain.


Sharpmap was born out of the need for solutions that were disappearing from the market. With our advanced technological background, we’ve managed to recreate the terrain's topography with exceptional accuracy, making trip planning easier.


This is just the beginning of our journey. We will continue to develop our tool, introducing new features to make it even more reliable and precise on the trail.
